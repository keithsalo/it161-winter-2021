About Amber

Amber Davenport was born and raised in Seattle, WA. Her passion for interior design began at a young age and she attributes drawing much of her inspiration from the natural world to the beautiful surroundings in the Pacific Northwest. She has worked with a variety of residential clients and caters her services to each personâ€™s specific needs.

Her first hands-on work experience began with visual merchandising in a retail setting. She attended Bellevue college and obtained an Associate in Arts Interior Studies degree. Upon completion of the program Amber offered design services at EcoHaus in Seattle where she became well versed in environmentally friendly building methods and materials. She later worked as a store manager / designer at Kasala Furniture which familiarized her with high end and custom furniture, lighting, and area rugs.

Amber returned to Bellevue college and received her Bachelor of Applied Arts in Interior Design. Eventually she became the furniture and accessories buyer at Kasala Furniture. This has allowed her to stay currently with industry trends and products. She continues to work with residential design clients
